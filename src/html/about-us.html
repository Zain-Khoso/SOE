Our Origin

Our Story

The Education has been destroyed by many well known factors in our society. It has changed the mental approach of students and parents that there is no way to success without following the all existing malpractices. Keeping above facts in view, It was thought by well wishers and senior educationist of Sukkur city to develop School system where quality education on affording cost should be provided and students/parents understanding should be changed that success can only be achieved by getting quality education with innovative ways without malpractices. With this vision the School of Excellence was established in 2006. School of Excellence started functioning on 15th August 2006 from class one to class five with seven teachers and 03 students, The number of student increased within short span of time and reached up to 63 students. The famous Educationist were the members of selection board for teachers of this school, They have decided that masters degree holders with 1st division from any HEC approved university will be selected as a teacher after passing the written test and demonstration. By the Grace of Almighty ALLAH, with these rules and regulations, hard working, Dedicated, Experienced teachers was appointed in this School. The admissions increased in every session and parents appreciation and demand has forced us to increase the capacity of school and also level from Montessori to College level, Now School of Excellence is having classes from P.G to Intermediate level and separate classes for Boys and Girls from class 4 to Intermediate. Our aim is to prepare students to compete on national and international level and Prove that quality education and hard working is the only key to success.

Our Mission

Mission of SOE is to establish, sustain and enhance itself as a quality-centric educational institution that provides excellent academic environment and opportunities for creating educated, productive, and responsible citizens of Pakistan through intellectual, and personal growth.
